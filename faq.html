Does the stopwatch keep running in the background?

Yes. Timers keep running while you browse other tools. Use the active timer bar to jump back instantly.

Will my countdown/alarm work offline?

After the first visit (or when installed), core pages are cached for offline access. Keep the tab open so alarms can trigger reliably (browser limitation).
